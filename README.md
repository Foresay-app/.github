# Foresay

  Social trading hub for perpetuals and prediction markets.
  Foresay routes orders to multiple non-custodial venues from one
  mobile-first interface, with creator-led rooms for collaborative
  trading and signal-sharing.

  ## What it is

  Foresay is a non-custodial trading client. The app does not hold
  user funds — keys remain in the user's wallet (Trust Wallet–style
  flow), and trades are executed directly against the underlying venue.

  - Perpetuals: Hyperliquid (live)
  - Prediction markets: Polymarket (in development)
  - Additional venues planned

  Each venue is rendered as a first-class terminal with its native
  order types and leverage rules preserved 1:1 — Foresay does not
  impose custom caps on top of the venue.

  ## Creator rooms

  Rooms are persistent group surfaces where creators publish
  positions, signals, and commentary alongside a shared chart. Members
  can mirror or react to trades while keeping their own wallets and
  risk in control.

  ## Status
